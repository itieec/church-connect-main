# ADR-004 — Chat Membership Independent of Team Membership

**Status:** Approved (Architecture Handbook v0.3)

## Decision

Chat membership is separate from team membership. Selected ministers from other teams may join a channel without receiving that team’s permissions or protected ministry-record access.

## Consequences

- Teams may have multiple chat channels
- Chat data remains organization-owned and isolated
- Parent channels (if any) do not grant local org data access
